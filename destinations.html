Top Destinations in India

Agra

Home to the iconic Taj Mahal, the epitome of love and a UNESCO World Heritage site.

Delhi

The vibrant capital city, blending ancient history, diverse cultures, and modernity.

Mumbai

The bustling financial capital, city of dreams, and heart of Bollywood.

Varanasi

The spiritual capital of India, known for its ancient ghats, vibrant culture, and sacred Ganges River.

Goa

Sun-kissed beaches, vibrant nightlife, and a unique blend of Indian and Portuguese cultures.

Kerala

God's Own Country - serene backwaters, lush greenery, and exotic wildlife.

Manali

A breathtaking hill station in the Himalayas, perfect for adventure and nature lovers.

Kedarnath

The sacred abode of Lord Shiva, a spiritual pilgrimage amidst majestic Himalayan peaks.

Ooty

The Queen of Hill Stations, known for its lush tea gardens, misty mountains, and colonial charm.
