Ooty

The Queen of Hill Stations | A Nilgiri Gem

Nestled in the Nilgiri Hills, Ooty is a verdant paradise of rolling tea plantations, serene lakes, and colonial charm. Breathe in the fresh mountain air and explore the breathtaking landscapes of this iconic South Indian hill station.

Discover Ooty's Beauty

Best Tourist Places in Ooty

  • Ooty Lake: Enjoy a peaceful boat ride or a stroll around this picturesque man-made lake.
  • Botanical Gardens: Explore a vast collection of exotic plants, flowers, and the famous fossilized tree trunk.
  • Doddabetta Peak: The highest point in the Nilgiris, offering stunning panoramic views of the surrounding hills.
  • Tea Museum & Factory: Learn about the process of tea making and sample fresh Nilgiri tea.
  • Nilgiri Mountain Railway: Take a ride on the UNESCO World Heritage toy train for a nostalgic and scenic journey.
  • Pykara Lake & Waterfalls: A serene spot perfect for boating and admiring the cascading waterfalls.

Travel Tips for Exploring Ooty

  • The best time to visit is from October to June, offering pleasant days and cool nights.
  • Pack layers of clothing, including sweaters and a light jacket, as the weather can change quickly.
  • Don't miss trying the local homemade chocolates and fresh bakery items.
  • Book your Nilgiri Mountain Railway tickets well in advance, especially during peak season, as they sell out fast.
  • Carry cash, as digital payments may not be available in more remote areas or smaller shops.
  • Explore the scenic drives to Coonoor and Kotagiri for more stunning tea garden views.
