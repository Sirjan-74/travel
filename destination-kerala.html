Kerala

God's Own Country | Backwaters & Beaches

Immerse yourself in the serene backwaters, lush tea plantations, and pristine beaches of Kerala. Renowned as 'God's Own Country', this South Indian paradise offers a unique blend of vibrant culture, rich history, and breathtaking natural beauty.

Top Attractions in Kerala

  • Alleppey (Alappuzha) Backwaters: Drift through tranquil canals on a traditional houseboat.
  • Munnar: Explore sprawling tea gardens, misty hills, and cascading waterfalls.
  • Kochi (Cochin): Discover colonial architecture, Chinese fishing nets, and vibrant art scenes.
  • Varkala Beach: Relax on stunning cliff-backed beaches, perfect for surfing and sunbathing.
  • Periyar National Park: Embark on a wildlife safari to spot elephants, tigers, and diverse flora.
  • Thrissur: Experience vibrant festivals, ancient temples, and rich cultural heritage.

Travel Tips for Kerala

  • Best time to visit: October to March for pleasant weather. Avoid monsoon (June-September) for backwater activities.
  • Book houseboats and resorts in advance, especially during peak season and holidays.
  • Taste authentic Kerala cuisine, including Sadhya (traditional meal) and fresh seafood.
  • Explore the culture with Kathakali performances or martial arts demonstrations.
  • Respect local traditions and dress modestly when visiting temples and religious sites.
  • Carry light cotton clothing, sunscreen, and insect repellent.
