Manali

Himalayan Adventure Hub

Nestled in the majestic Himalayas, Manali is a breathtaking resort town in Himachal Pradesh. It's a paradise for adventure seekers, a haven for nature lovers, and a gateway to the stunning Lahaul and Spiti valleys. Discover snow-capped peaks, gushing rivers, and vibrant culture.

Plan Your Manali Adventure

Top Attractions in Manali

  • Hadimba Devi Temple: A unique wooden temple amidst a cedar forest, dedicated to Goddess Hadimba.
  • Solang Valley: The adventure hub for paragliding, zorbing, skiing, and magnificent views.
  • Rohtang Pass: A high mountain pass offering unparalleled views of glaciers and peaks (seasonal access).
  • Old Manali: Experience the bohemian vibe, quaint cafes, and local handicrafts.
  • Beas River: Enjoy riverside strolls, white-water rafting, and the serene beauty of the gushing river.
  • Vashisht Village: Famous for its hot springs and ancient temples, offering therapeutic dips.

Travel Tips for Manali

  • Best time to visit: April to June for adventure sports, October to February for snow.
  • Rohtang Pass access is restricted; check permits and road conditions in advance.
  • Carry heavy woolens, especially if visiting during winter or going to higher altitudes.
  • Stay hydrated and take it easy on your first day to acclimatize.
  • Respect local culture and traditions when visiting temples and villages.
  • Explore beyond the main town to discover hidden gems like Malana or Kasol.
